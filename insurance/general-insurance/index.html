General Insurance

Our general insurance solutions offer robust protection for your assets, shielding you from unforeseen risks and giving you confidence in your coverage.

Fire Insurance

Provides coverage for damage to buildings, contents, and stocks caused by fire, lightning, or explosion, ensuring financial security.

Burglary Insurance

Protects homes and businesses against theft or forced entry damage to insured property, offering peace of mind and stability.

Marine Insurance

Offers coverage for goods transported by sea, air, or land under Marine Cargo and protects ships or boats against damage under Marine Hull.

Engineering Insurance

Includes Contractors’ All Risk for construction projects, Erection All Risk for machinery installations, Machinery Breakdown for mechanical failures, and Electronic Equipment Insurance for IT and electronic devices.

Liability Insurance

Provides Public Liability coverage for third-party injury or damage claims, Employers’ Liability for employee-related claims, Professional Indemnity for professionals like doctors and lawyers, and Directors & Officers Liability to shield executives from legal claims.

Work Injury Benefits Act (WIBA) Insurance

Ensures employees are covered against workplace injuries, disabilities, and occupational diseases, providing compliance with labor laws and financial security.

Domestic Package Insurance

Protects residential homes against fire, burglary, liability claims, and personal accidents, ensuring homeowners and families remain financially secure.

Personal Accident Insurance

Provides financial compensation in the event of accidental injury, disability, or death, ensuring income protection and peace of mind.

Travel Insurance

Covers medical emergencies, lost luggage, trip cancellations, and other travel risks, ensuring a smooth and worry-free journey for travelers.

Agriculture Insurance

Provides Crop Insurance to cover weather-related losses and Livestock Insurance to protect against loss due to disease, theft, or accidents.

Goods in Transit Insurance

Offers financial protection for goods against damage or loss while being transported, ensuring business continuity and security.

Money Insurance

Covers businesses against financial loss of cash in transit or on-premises due to theft, robbery, or unexpected incidents.

Fidelity Guarantee Insurance

Protects businesses from financial losses caused by employee fraud, dishonesty, or embezzlement, ensuring long-term trust.
